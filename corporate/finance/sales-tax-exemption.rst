Sales tax exemption
===================

.. vale Google.Passive = NO
.. vale Google.We = NO
.. vale Google.Will = NO
.. vale write-good.E-Prime = NO
.. vale write-good.Passive = NO

Purchases made directly by Georgia Tech, using an Institute procurement card or purchase order, are exempt from sales tax.
See the `Procurement & Business Services website <https://procurement.gatech.edu/payables/taxinfo#taxexempt>`_ for forms to provide to the supplier.

.. important::
   Purchases made by RoboJackets via :ref:`Ramp <corporate/key-vendors:Ramp>` or any other payment method generally **aren't** exempt, and we must pay sales tax, even if the purchase will be reimbursed by Georgia Tech.

   **If and only if the purchase is for resale only, a corporation officer can provide a certificate of exemption to the supplier.**
   Students **don't** have authorization to sign on behalf of the corporation.
   See :ref:`Purchasing merchandise <corporate/merchandise-sales:Purchasing merchandise>` for details.

.. note::
   Some vendor accounts may already have a sales tax exemption applied.
   If you're purchasing using a Ramp card, ensure sales tax is charged correctly.
